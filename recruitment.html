FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Do I need any experience?
No experience is needed at all! We'll supply all the equipment and teach you everything you need to know about the basics of dragon boat!

What if I don't know how to swim?

That's okay! Even though dragon boat is a water sport, being able to swim is not a requirement. Everyone on the boat is required to wear a personal flotation device (P.F.D) that the team will provide.

Is there a tryout process?

Due to the limited number of spots on our boats, we unfortunately have to hold tryouts. Tryouts are hosted during Week One and Week Two of Fall Quarter so stayed tuned for Fall 2018 Recruitment!

What is the time commitment?

As a competitive team, we have mandatory water practices, gym/conditioning sessions, social events, and meetings during the training season. During this season, you are expected to commit anywhere from 15-20 hours a week to the sport.
